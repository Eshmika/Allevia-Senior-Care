Independent Contractor Agreement

This Caregiver Independent Contractor Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into by and between Allevia, an Ohio LLC, doing business as Allevia Senior Care, and the Caregiver.
Allevia and the Caregiver desire to enter into an independent contractor relationship, in which the Caregiver desires and is able to provide and/or perform services for Allevia clients under the terms and conditions stated herein.

1. Definitions:

  • 1.1 “Service” - any and all work, services, tasks and/or care being provided and/or performed by any Allevia Independent Caregiver as described in any applicable Care Plan; and all other obligations as required by this Agreement, and any other such tasks that are deemed necessary by a Allevia Representative.
  • 1.2 “Client” - shall mean any person requiring Service and/or is financially responsible for person requiring Service; and has entered into an agreement with Allevia.
  • 1.3 "Confidential Information" - means all confidential or proprietary information, including without limitation: information not generally known to the public, Caregiver information, Client information and Allevia information.
  • 1.4 “Holiday” - New Year’s Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, and any other specific Federal and/or State holiday recognized by Allevia.
  • 1.5 “Expenses” - Expenses include, but are not limited to: groceries, parking fees, excessive mileage driven by CAREGIVER, bus fare, admissions, tips, and prescription payments made for and on behalf of the Client.

2. Independent Contractor Status:

  • 2.1 As Independent Contractor for Allevia, you will utilize your training and expertise to provide high quality care giving services for Allevia clients. For a period of time and under conditions that client and contractor independent determine. You may decline any Allevia assignment and may choose to complete as many as few Allevia assignment as you desire.
  • 2.2 You are an independent contractor. Nothing in this agreement is intended or should be construed, to create a partnership agency, joint venture or employment relationship. Allevia will not supervise, direct or control you in the manner or method of performing any Allevia assignment. Other than minimal performance expectations noted. You many be engaged or employed in any other business, trade, profession, or other activity which does not place you in breach of this agreement. You have no authority to bind Allevia to any contract or agreement and should not make any agreements or representations on behalf of Allevia without Allevia’s prior written consent.
  • 2.3 You will receive an IRS 1099-MISC from Allevia and will be solely responsible for payment of all taxes and insurance requirements under existing law. Allevia will not be responsible for withholding or paying any income, payroll, social security or other federal, state, or local taxes, making any insurance contributions, including unemployment or disability or obtaining workers' compensation insurance on your behalf. You shall indemnify Allevia against ALL such taxes or contributions, including penalties and interest and any all claims, liabilities, costs, or expenses that may arise out of any breach of this requirement, including any breach by your employees or contractors.
  • 2.4 As an independent contractor to Allevia, you will not be eligible to participate in any vacation, group medical or life insurance, disability, profit sharing or retirement benefits or any other fringe benefit or benefits plans offered by Allevia to its employees. You are not covered by any works’ compensation insurance Allevia provides to its direct employees, and you expressly waive any such coverage as a condition of this agreement. You are not eligible for unemployment benefits by virtue of this agreement with Allevia. You agree to maintain adequate workers’ compensation, commercial general liability, errors and omissions and other forms of insurance reasonably acceptable to Allevia. Each policy shall have a limit sufficient to protect and indemnify Allevia and its affiliates. If requested, you shall list Allevia as an additional insured under such policy. Allevia may request and you agree to furnish a certificate of insurance verifying such insurance.

3. SERVICE REQUIREMENTS:

  • 3.1 Control. As contractor of Allevia you are required to provide high quality care and or services to Allevia clients. Allevia will not supervise, direct, or control the manner or method in which you perform any assignment for an Allevia client.
  • 3.2 Compensation. The rate of pay for assignments may vary based on the contractual arrangements between Allevia and its clients and you may accept or decline any assignment offered.
  • 3.3. Notification. You shall advise Allevia if you ‘re not present for any assignment at least 15 minutes before the schedules start time. You shall give Allevia a 24 hours’ notice in advance for your inability to attend any accepted appointment. You understand that payment for your services may be withheld if you fail to follow this punctuality guidelines. You agree to clock in and out or provide a confirmation assigned via email or scheduling system within 24 hours after each assignment in order to get paid on time. You understand that you many not attend any assignment on behalf of Allevia without authorization from Allevia and that you will not be eligible for payment for any services rendered without proper authorization.
  • 3.4 Performance. In performing any service on behalf of Allevia, you shall dress professionally and follow the dress code of Allevia’s client. You shall not perform any service for an Allevia client while under the influence of illegal drugs or alcohol. You are responsible for any and all costs and expenses incurred related to performance of any service for Allevia.
  • 3.5 Changes. Allevia assignment may be cancelled or modified with little to no notice. You agree to check you email daily. You agree to check your email before you departing to the assignments to confirm that the assignment still available. You agree to not hold Allevia responsible if an assignment is cancelled via e-mail or other communication method, and you have not checked your communication method.

4. Term and Termination:

  • 4.1 Term. When fully executed by both parties, this Agreement commences on the Effective Date set forth above, and shall remain in effect indefinitely, until terminated in accordance with the provisions herein (the “Term”).
  • 4.2 Termination. Either party may terminate this Agreement, upon written notice, without cause; and such termination shall be effective ten (10) business days from the delivery of such notice. This Agreement may be terminated immediately by Allevia, for cause, if in its sole and absolute discretion, Allevia determines that any of the following events has occurred:
    a. CAREGIVER becomes abusive in any way to and/or endangers the health or safety of the Client.
    b. CAREGIVER materially breaches any term or condition of this Agreement.
    c. CAREGIVER violates any of the expectations, guidelines, or the policies and procedures of Allevia as identified in Orientation.
    d. Upon mutual consent of both parties.

    CONFIDENTIALITY: You understand that during this agreement you will have access to confidential and proprietary information of Allevia and its clients, without limitation customer list, vendor/supplier lists, trade secrets, methods and processes involving in providing services, financial data, pricing histories, Pricing strategies, pricing margins and other information and knowledge relating to the business. You acknowledge that such confidential information is the sole and exclusive property of Allevia and maintaining that confidentiality is essential to Allevia ability to conduct its business. During the term of this agreement or at anytime you will not directly or indirectly communicate or divulge any confidential information to any third party. Each party shall, however, be permitted to disclose relevant aspects of the other party's Confidential Information to its officers, agents, subcontractors and employees to the extent that such disclosure is reasonably necessary for the performance of its duties and obligations under this Agreement.
